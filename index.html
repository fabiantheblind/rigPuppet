rigPuppet by fabiantheblind

some JavaScripts for Aobe After Effects (CS4) using costum effects to rig a puppet

These javascripts are written for a specific workflow. Feel free to use and abuse them. This is not ready made and could need some coustmizing.

Dependencies

After Effects CS4

Install

Place the whole download in After Effects CSX/Scripts/ Use the puppet provided with the download to setup your project.
